# Comprehensive Sales and Profit Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This repository contains a dynamic, multi-year **Sales and Profit Dashboard** built entirely in Microsoft Excel. The project analyzes e-commerce transactional data across four years (2021–2024) to provide key business stakeholders with deep, actionable insights into total revenue, profitability drivers, geographical distribution, and seasonal trends.

## Interactive Features Included
* **Dynamic Slicers:** Interactive filtering by Product Category, Calendar Year, and Month for granular analysis.
* **Geographical Mapping:** Integrated regional map charting top-performing states.
* **Product Performance Funnel:** Visual funnel breakdown highlighting high-velocity product categories.

---

The dashboard highlights the following critical business performance indicators:

## Detailed Visual Analysis

### 1. Product Category & Profit Breakdown (Funnel & Cluster Chart)
* **Top Drivers:** Phones ($279,464) and Chairs ($277,059) lead total company sales by a significant margin.
* **Profitability by Year:** Technology consistently emerges as a primary profit driver, peaking sharply in fiscal year 2023 before adjusting down in 2024. 
* **Underperformers:** Fasteners, Labels, and Envelopes occupy the lowest tier of sales velocity, suggesting room for inventory optimization.

### 2. Seasonality and Trends (Sales By Month)
* **Q4 Surge:** There is a distinct, recurring seasonal spike during the holiday quarters. Sales hit a major seasonal high in **December ($241.5k)** and **November ($234.0k)**.
* **Q1 Reset:** Sales drop significantly post-holidays, hitting the lowest annual baseline in **February ($59.6k)**.
